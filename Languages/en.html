Welcome to the local unit of the world's largest humanitarian network

Discover the Red Cross of Nantes: an organization dedicated to alleviating suffering and assisting the most vulnerable. Committed to humanity, impartiality, and compassion, we respond to local needs efficiently. Since our inception, we have provided emergency relief, food aid, social support, and first aid training. With the support of our volunteers and partners, we are building a supportive future for all.

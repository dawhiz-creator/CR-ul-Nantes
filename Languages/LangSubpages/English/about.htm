Who Are We?

The Red Cross of Nantes is a local humanitarian organization dedicated to your well-being. Our mission is to accompany you and your loved ones by providing personalized support services, whatever your needs. We offer a wide range of services tailored to each situation.

Personalized Help for All!

Because everyone has unique needs, at the Nantes Red Cross, we offer customized solutions. Whether you need regular support, specific medical services, or special accompaniment, we tailor our services to meet your expectations.

Feel free to contact us: our team will be happy to discuss your needs and offer you a suitable solution.

Our Teams Are By Your Side.

"Every person deserves personalized support. We are committed to providing this close service."

We do everything possible to make it happen. This is also reflected in our constant availability and our commitment to the quality of the services we provide.
